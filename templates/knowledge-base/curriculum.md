For each topic you care about, decide a depth target and edit it in below.
Suggested levels:

- **survey** — one or two pages, just enough to recognise the shape.
- **working** — enough breadth to reason about it; multiple sources cross-checked.
- **deep** — primary literature read; you can defend positions and notice contradictions.

Remove this guidance once your own list takes shape.
